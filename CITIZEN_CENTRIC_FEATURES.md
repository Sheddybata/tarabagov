### 1. **Centralized Digital Records System**

#### Schools Directory
- **Features:**
  - Searchable database of all schools (public/private)
  - School profiles: location, contact, facilities, enrollment data
  - Performance metrics and accreditation status
  - Admission information and requirements
  - Eliminate ghost schools through verification

#### Hospitals Directory
- **Features:**
  - Complete list of healthcare facilities
  - Services offered, specialties, equipment
  - Operating hours and emergency contacts
  - Bed availability (real-time)
  - Doctor schedules and appointments

#### MDA Directory
- **Features:**
  - All ministries, departments, and agencies
  - Contact information and office locations
  - Services offered by each MDA
  - Leadership and staff directory
  - Service delivery timelines
